Clickmate BETA

Hey guys! We are looking for people who would like to test out new versions of the Clickmate before they will be published in Google Play.

This time in BETA version we have added possibility to record and reproduce touch events on expanded status bar and probably on most of floating widgets. There still no possibility to record touch events on navigation bar and status bar, but for expanding notifications you can use swipe to down gesture which is supported in most launchers (like Lawnchair and Nova Launcher). What is more, app does not need permission to draw over other apps anymore.



Therefore if you want to receive beta versions and give us some feedback after testing it out, simply use button below to join Firebase Beta Distribution - we will provide beta version as soon as it will be ready.

Thank you! Clickmate Team
